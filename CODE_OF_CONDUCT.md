We expect all LF BB employees, member companies, and participants to abide by our
[Code of
Conduct](https://www.linuxfoundation.org/code-of-conduct).

If you are being harassed, notice that someone else is being harassed, or have
any other concerns involving someone’s welfare, please notify a member of the
LF BB team or email [conduct@lfbroadband.org](mailto:conduct@lfbroadband.org).
